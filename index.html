Food for your thoughts.

Traveling this holiday season? See how you can help burst our bubbles, connect with strangers, and learn from communities that aren't like ours.

America is disconnected.

More and more, it feels like we're living in bubbles. How can we engage with and heal a country we don't understand?

So here's the plan.

  1. Grab some candy.
  2. Go to Main Street, or the grocery store, or wherever people are.
  3. Exchange a piece of candy for a hope.
  4. Listen and empathize.

Ready? Let's do it.

Commit to talk to strangers, and challenge your friends to do so too.

Have some materials.

Buy a t-shirt. Print a sign. Make a sandwichboard. How you do this is up to you.

Be prepared.

Conversations could veer into uncomfortable territory. Try to listen, don't try to argue or engage. Our goal is not to change minds, it is merely to try to understand them.

Share what you've heard.

Have a good conversation? Let the world know! Tweet or Instagram with #saysomethingeatsomething. Be sure not to violate the privacy of anyone you've met.
